AC Valhall: Wrath of the Druids - 1st impressions

So far I've been having a good time with this dlc. I may be slightly biased, living in the city where you first step ashore. Apart from the novelty factor of trying to find the rough location of my flat in the 9th Century game world,... it does seem like simply more of what Assassin's Creed Valhalla already had to offer, with some variety added by the new druidic faction & their hallucinogenic form of combat. That may be a good or a bad thing depending on where you stand. I did find myself getting worn out by a lot of the busy work in the previous entry, Odyssey. I'm finding nothing that's putting me off so far, so we'll see how it goes when I get around to a full review.
